How to Play

Play the Game!


This a game which tests your nerve. Roll the pigs to accumulate points. Stick whenever you like to bank your points! BUT... if you roll a 'Pig Out' you will lose all of your points for this round. If your pigs decide to 'Make Bacon', your total points will go back to ZERO!

Can you be the first player to reach 100 points and win the game??
